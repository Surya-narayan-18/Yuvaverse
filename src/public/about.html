How We Got Here

Our Journey So Far

What started as a small group of five passionate students has grown into a movement that touches thousands of lives every semester.

2022

Founded

Five students with a shared dream founded Yuvaverse in a college canteen. The first meeting had 12 attendees.

2022 — First Event

TechTalks 1.0

Our first major event — a tech symposium with 6 speakers and 200+ student attendees. It was chaos, but beautiful chaos.

2023

Scaling Up

Hosted 15+ events across 2 campuses. Launched the cultural fest "Yuvarang" with 600+ registrations.

2024

2000+ Milestone

Crossed 2,000 total attendees. Partnered with 10+ campus organizations and secured our first industry sponsorship.

2025 — Now

Going Digital

Launched this platform for seamless event discovery, online registrations, and secure payments. The best is yet to come.
